Let $k\in \ZZ^{+}$ a complex sequence $(u_{n})_{n\geq0}$ is said to be satisfy a Linear reccurrence of order $k$ if there are fixed complex numbers $b_{1},\dots b_{k}\st$ 
$$
\begin{align}
		u_{n+k}  & = b_{1}u_{n}+\dots b_{k} y_{n+k}  \forall n\geq0
\end{align}
$$
