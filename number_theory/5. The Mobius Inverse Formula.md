> [!definition] The Mobius Inverse Formula
> let $f$ , $F$ be arthmetic function then
> $F(n)=\sum_{\frac{d}{n}}f(d)\iff f(n)=\sum_{d|n}F(d)\mu\left( \frac{n}{d} \right)$

^e5d0e5

`\begin{proof}`
$$
\begin{align}
F & =f*u \\
\iff F*\mu & =(f*u)(\mu) \\
 & =f*\mu*u \\
 & = f*I =f
\end{align}
$$
